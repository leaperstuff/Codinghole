What are Text Elements?

In HTML, there are various types of text elements that help structure content. Common text elements include:

  • <h1>: The main heading of a page.
  • <p>: A paragraph of text.
  • <span>: A section of text within another element.
  • <strong>: Text that is strongly emphasized, often displayed in bold.
  • <em>: Text that is emphasized, often displayed in italics.
  • <ul>: An unordered list.
  • <ol>: An ordered list.
